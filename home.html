Dr. Rajesh Kumar Mundotiya is an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology (Bhilai), India, where he brings a wealth of experience and expertise in computer science and engineering. Dr. Mundotiya holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Varanasi, obtained in 2022, and a B.Tech-M.Tech (IDD) in Information and Communication Technology from the Centre for Converging Technologies, University of Rajasthan, Jaipur, completed in 2015. His research interests encompass a broad spectrum, including Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, and Automatic Speech Processing.

Dr. Mundotiya's expertise extends to cutting-edge areas such as Dialogue Generation, Image Captioning, and Machine Translation, where he leverages his knowledge to drive innovation. Moreover, Dr. Mundotiya's interdisciplinary approach integrates medical, psychological, and cognitive sciences insights into NLP applications to enhance technology's impact on healthcare and mental health.
